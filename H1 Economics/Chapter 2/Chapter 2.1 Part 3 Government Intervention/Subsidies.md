# Subsidies
## Indirect subsidy
A grant given by the government to encourage consumption or production of good or services 

### Specific Subsidy
Causes a parallel rightward shift of supply curve.

### Ad valorem subsidy
Causes a rightward pivot of supply curve about origin.
